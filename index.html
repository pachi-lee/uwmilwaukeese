Lec 8 · Design & Databases
Cohesion vs Coupling
Cohesion — focus of one module; HIGH is desirable
Coupling — dependence between modules; LOW is desirable

Abstraction (up, general) vs Refinement (down, specific)
automobile → car → sports car → Chevy Corvette
Relational DB
Table = rows + columns (uniform type per column)
PK — uniquely identifies each row; duplicate → exception
FK — holds PK from another table
Many-to-One: FK on "many" side
Many-to-Many: junction table (2 FKs)
ORM: Django · SQLAlchemy · Hibernate
